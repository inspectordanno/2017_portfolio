My adventures in D3

I attempted to teach myself D3 in 2016 using Scott Murray's very handy book. I wasn't quite able to load-in custom data, but I did succeed in building a couple of basic charts that update with random data. I am currently enrolled in a D3 course in grad school, where I am visualizing data from the Titanic disaster. More to come!
