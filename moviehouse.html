A website about my Dad's Philadelphia history business

This was the first website I made from scratch, ever! My Dad runs a business about Philadelphia nostaglia, and I needed to develop a website. Could the stars have aligned any better?

I realize now that I could have made some better design choices. For example, there is no coherence in the typography. Also, there is no color scheme. When I have a break in classes, I will revisit this site and improve it with my new design knowledge! I am proud of the website, however. I think it has a simple information architecture and conveys what my Dad does very fluently.
